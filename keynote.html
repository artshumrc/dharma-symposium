Opening Keynote Panel

Reflection on “Dharma and Puṇya: Buddhist Ritual Art” and the later history of Nepal

Chair: Eugene Wang, Harvard University
Jinah Kim & Todd Lewis

5:30 – 6:30pm

The co-curators of the exhibition “Dharma and Puṇya” will reflect on the exhibition’s making and curation, including their aspirations and goals, along with some of the challenges encountered along the way.

 

 

LAKṢACAITYA VRATA, dated 1808 (N.S. 929), Kathmandu, Nepal, Asian Art Museum, San Francisco
